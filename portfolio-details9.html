History

Landhuis Tamboen, now known as Gedung Juang, is one of the historical buildings in Bekasi Regency. This building was built in a mixture of European and Chinese styles. This combination of styles is called compradoric. Developed towards the end of the 19th century and the beginning of the 20th century.
