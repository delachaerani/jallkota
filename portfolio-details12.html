Attractiveness

This beach has not been managed professionally as a tourist attraction. However, the seaside area offers many beautiful views that spoil the eye. Mekar Beach has a different panorama compared to other beaches. If generally tourist beaches have straight or concave coastlines, this tourist attraction in Mekar Beach Village is actually convex in shape. The water is also quite clear. In addition to panoramic sea views, visitors can see the estuary of the Gembong River, which is a tributary of the Citarum river. Another attraction on the beach is the joglo villas. Villajoglo is a gazebo placed in the middle of the sea. The building is made of wood, bamboo and leaves as a roof. The place is a suitable point for fishing. Visitors also often use the gazebo as a picnic or meeting place.
