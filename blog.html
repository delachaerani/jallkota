Santa Clara Church

Santa Clara Church, located in Bekasi City's North Bekasi District, serves as a place of worship for Catholics. Because the congregation numbers over 8000 people, Sunday mass is held four times.

Located at: Jl. Lkr. Utara, RT.03/RW.06, Harapan Baru, Kec. North Bekasi, Bekasi city, West Java 17123 Phone: (021) 8870327

Hok Lay Kiong Temple

Hok Lay Kiong Temple is the city's oldest temple. Referring to the Hok Lay Kiong Temple's history, which dates back more than 300 years and is still active today. Hindus, Taoists, and Confucians all worship at this temple.

Address: Jl. Kenari I No.1, RT.006/RW.001, Ds. Margahayu, Kec. East Bekasi, Bekasi city, West Java.
