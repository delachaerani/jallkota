Attractiveness

It is one of the largest malls in Bekasi City with the theme "World of shopping and recreation" and is located close to the city's toll gate. And become one of the city's defining symbols. This shopping center includes two wings with dining and fashion lifestyle establishments. In addition, the Metropolitan Mall's leisure rides at the very top are extremely spacious, making it ideal for individuals who wish to bring their families, particularly their elementary-aged children. In addition, the Horizon Hotel, a four-star hotel, is directly attached to the Metropolitan Mall, so hotel guests who wish to visit the mall do not have to drive far.
