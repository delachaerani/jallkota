About

Bekasi has a Go! water park. Wet Waterpark. This 7.5 hectare water park is located at Grand Wisata Bekasi, Tambun. The concepts that are carried out are entertainment, edutaiment and sportaiment. Exhibiting various child-friendly water attractions, Go! Wet Waterpark is also targeting the millennial market. "Millennials need adrenaline. We have the only challenging slide in Asia, then there is a Wave Pool with eight types of waves," said Herjanto Judarta, director of Mahaka Attraction, Thursday (20/2/2020) in Relaunching Go! Wet Waterpark.
